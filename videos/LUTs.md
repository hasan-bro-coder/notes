## Intro: 
have you ever wanted to make dull colors in your game pop. well meet LUTs , Look Up Table. 
## body:
think of them as color filter for your game,with a look up table you can map a color to a different color for example,changing this specific red color to green then every pixel that has that shade of red becomes green. here are some example.now to use a look up table in godot you can download a base LUT from godot documentation then take a screenshot of your game and make the desired color corrections in the software of your choice then apply the same filters to the LUT and then import the LUT in godot as a texture 3d then in the worldenvironment node assign the LUT or you can use this shader if you want to use multiple LUTs and change the LUTs in runtime
